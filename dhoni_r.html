Records of MS Dhoni

Indian Team Achievement under Dhoni's Captaincy

  1. India won the 2007 ICC World Twenty20
  2. The Asia Cups in 2010 and 2016
  3. ICC Cricket World Cup, 2011 and
  4. ICC Champions Trophy 2013.

ODI Records of Dhoni

  1. Dhoni is the third captain (and the first non-Australian) overall to win 100 games.
  2. Most career runs in ODI history when batting at number 6 position (4031).
  3. First Indian and fifth overall to hit 200 sixes in ODIs.
  4. Dhoni holds the records of the most dismissals in an innings (6) and career (432) by an Indian wicket-keeper.
  5. Dhoni has the most stumpings (123) by any wicket-keeper in an ODI career.
  6. First Indian wicket-keeper to take 300 ODI catches and fourth wicket-keeper in the world who achieved this milestone.
  7. The first player to pass 10,000 runs in ODI cricket with having a career average of over 50.
  8. He is the only player to score more than one hundred in ODI cricket when batting at number 7 position or lower (Dhoni has 2 centuries at number 7).
  9. Most not outs in ODI history (84).

Test match Records of Dhoni

  1. Under M.S. Dhoni's captaincy, the Indian team topped the Test cricket rankings for the first time in 2009.
  2. With 27 Test wins, Dhoni is the most successful Indian Test captain, surpassing Sourav Ganguly's record of 21 wins.
  3. M.S. Dhoni is the first Indian wicket-keeper to complete 4,000 test runs.
  4. M.S.Dhoni's 224 against Australia in Chennai is the third-highest score by an Indian captain.
  5. M.S.Dhoni, with 294 dismissals in his career, ranks first in the all-time dismissals list by Indian wicket-keepers.
  6. Dhoni's maiden century against Pakistan in Faisalabad (148) is the fastest century scored by an Indian wicket-keeper, and fourth overall.

T20 Records of Dhoni

  1. As a captain most wins (41) in T20Is.
  2. Most matches (72) both as captain and wicket-keeper in T20Is
  3. Most dismissals (87) as wicket-keeper in T20Is.
  4. Most consecutive T20I innings (84) without a duck.
  5. Most catches (54) as wicket-keeper in T20Is.

IPL Records of Dhoni

  1. Half-century batting at five different position in the IPL
  2. Only player to hit more than 600+ runs in 19th and 20th over in the IPL
  3. Highest runs in death overs in IPL(2500+)
  4. 4th in Most sixes list in IPL(235*)
  5. Only Captain to win 100+ games in the history of the IPL
  6. Dhoni lead CSK in playoffs 9 times out of 11 and won 4 trophies
